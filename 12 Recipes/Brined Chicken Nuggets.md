🍗 Brined Chicken Nuggets

### **Ingredients**
**The Brine**
*   **Water:** 4 cups cold water
*   **Salt (Choose one):**
    *   6 tbsp Diamond Crystal Kosher
    *   *OR* 4 ½ tbsp Morton’s Kosher
    *   *OR* 3 tbsp Table Salt
*   **Optional Aromatics:** Garlic, Dill, etc.

**The Breading**
*   1 ½ cups flour
*   1 ½ cups plain bread crumbs
*   1 tbsp garlic powder
*   1 tbsp kosher salt
*   2 tsp black pepper
*   Paprika & Powdered Sugar (to taste)

---

### **Instructions**

**1. Prepare the Brine**
Mix the water and your chosen salt together. Stir until fully dissolved (this may take 1–2 minutes). Add any optional aromatics now.

**2. Marinate**
Place chicken chunks into a large zip-top bag with the brine mixture. Refrigerate for at least **1 hour**.

**3. Prepare the Breading**
In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, bread crumbs, garlic powder, salt, pepper, paprika, and powdered sugar.

**4. Coat & Cook**
*   Drain the chicken from the brine. 
*   Working in batches, coat each piece thoroughly in the breading mixture (ensure all sides are covered).
*   **Air Fry:** Cook at **400°F for 8–10 minutes**.
*   **Note:** Flip or shake the basket halfway through cooking.
