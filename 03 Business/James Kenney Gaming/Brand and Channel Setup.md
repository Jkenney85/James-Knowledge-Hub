# Brand and channel setup

## Working brand

- **Channel:** James Kenney
- **Series label:** 100% Completion
- **Tone:** Calm, methodical, nostalgic, honest
- **Proof point:** No cheats. No save states. Every collectible.

## Channel description

> Mostly uncut 100% completion runs of classic and modern games. Each episode has a clear objective, visible checklist progress, and a planned route—without cheats or save states.
>
> New long-form gameplay every Sunday, with Shorts highlighting discoveries, close calls, and major milestones.
>
> Current series: The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time on the original Nintendo 64 version.

## Reusable series description

> This is a planned 100% completion run of The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time for Nintendo 64, played through emulation. The run uses no cheats and no save states. Each episode focuses on a specific objective while tracking story progress, Heart Pieces, Gold Skulltulas, equipment, upgrades, songs, and side quests.

## Visual direction

- Dark forest green, warm gold, parchment cream
- One serif display font plus one highly readable sans-serif
- Large objective text; minimal small copy
- Use original gameplay screenshots you captured
- Avoid implying that the channel is official or sponsored by Nintendo

## Title pattern

`[Specific Goal] | Ocarina of Time 100% — Part [##]`

Examples:

- `Inside the Great Deku Tree | Ocarina of Time 100% — Part 01`
- `Dodongo's Cavern and Every Early Upgrade | Ocarina of Time 100% — Part 02`
- `Completing the Forest Temple | Ocarina of Time 100% — Part 04`

Do not lead every title with `Part 01`. The goal is the clickable idea; the episode number provides order.

## Silent calls to action

Use no more than one per episode:

- `Following the full 100% run? Subscribe for the next objective.`
- `Next Sunday: [specific goal]`
- `Full series playlist in the description`
