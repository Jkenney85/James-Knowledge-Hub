# Content calendar

## Default publishing rhythm

- **Sunday:** Long-form episode
- **Tuesday:** Short 1 — discovery moment
- **Thursday:** Short 2 — progress, tip, close call, or payoff

Only publish a second Short when the clip is genuinely strong. One useful Short beats two filler clips.

## Weekly production calendar

The available budget is approximately 18 hours per week.

| Day | Time | Focus |
|---|---:|---|
| Monday | 2 hr | Route and episode goal; prep overlays |
| Tuesday | 2 hr | Record part 1 |
| Wednesday | 2 hr | Record part 2 or finish the objective |
| Thursday | 2 hr | Edit dead time; add cards and chapters |
| Friday | 2 hr | Thumbnail, title, description, export |
| Saturday | 4 hr | Full quality check, upload, Short 1 |
| Sunday | 4 hr | Publish, comments, analytics, Short 2, next-week buffer |

If the episode is already complete, use the weekend time to build a one-week buffer rather than adding unnecessary polish.
