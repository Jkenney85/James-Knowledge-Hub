# Production workflow

## 1. Plan

- Choose one viewer-readable episode goal.
- Mark the relevant items in [[Games/Ocarina of Time/100 Percent Checklist]].
- List required route steps in order.
- Identify two or three likely Short moments.
- Prepare opening and results cards before recording.

## 2. Record

- Record original gameplay and game audio.
- Do not use cheats or save states.
- Start with enough pre-roll to avoid clipping the opening.
- Make a timestamp note when something notable happens.
- End only after the stated goal is met or a clear stopping point is reached.

## 3. Edit

The episode is mostly uncut, not untouched.

Remove:

- Emulator and desktop setup
- Crashes or technical interruptions
- Private notifications
- Very long idle periods
- Repeated failed attempts that add no new value

Add:

- 5–8 second opening goal card
- Current completion percentage or category progress
- Brief route or item notes when they help the viewer
- Milestone cards after major accomplishments
- YouTube chapter markers
- Final results card with items gained and next goal

## 4. Package

- Title names the game, specific goal, 100% promise, and episode number.
- Thumbnail communicates one accomplishment at phone size.
- Description explains the run rules and episode result.
- Add the video to the series playlist.
- Add a related-video end screen.

## 5. Quality check

- Watch the first 60 seconds without skipping.
- Scrub every chapter transition.
- Confirm gameplay and text are readable on a phone.
- Check that game audio never clips.
- Check title and thumbnail against the actual episode.
- Upload privately and wait for copyright checks before scheduling.

## 6. Publish and learn

- Publish Sunday at a consistent time.
- Reply to comments without spoiling later episodes.
- Record 48-hour and 7-day data in [[Analytics Tracker]].
- Pick exactly one improvement for the next episode.

## Overlay vocabulary

Keep silent editorial text brief and consistent:

- `TODAY'S GOAL`
- `ROUTE NOTE`
- `NEW ITEM`
- `CHECKLIST`
- `PROGRESS`
- `GOAL COMPLETE`
- `NEXT EPISODE`

Avoid full paragraphs during gameplay. The overlay should help, not compete with the game.
