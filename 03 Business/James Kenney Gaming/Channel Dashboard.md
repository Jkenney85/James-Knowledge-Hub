Complete, mostly uncut 100% game runs with clear episode goals, visible progress, and no cheats or save states.

The targets are planning markers, not forecasts. Subscriber and watch-hour trends matter more than hitting an arbitrary number on a particular date.

## North-star metrics

1. Valid public watch hours from long-form episodes
2. Returning viewers
3. Subscribers gained per long-form video
4. First 30-second retention
5. Impressions click-through rate

Shorts views are a discovery metric. Shorts-feed watch time does not count toward the 4,000-hour long-form threshold.
