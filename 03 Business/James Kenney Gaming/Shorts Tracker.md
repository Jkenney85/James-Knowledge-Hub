# Shorts tracker

## Clip criteria

A Short should contain a complete moment:

- A recognizable discovery
- A difficult item or collectible
- A close call or recovery
- A boss payoff
- A useful route tip expressed with text
- A major 100% milestone

## Short structure

- **0–1 sec:** Show the moment or state the challenge.
- **1–20 sec:** Build context with concise text.
- **Final seconds:** Deliver the item, discovery, win, or result.
- **End text:** `Full 100% run linked below`

Use the related-video feature to connect the Short to the exact long-form episode.

## Guardrails

- Use only your own recorded gameplay.
- Do not crop so tightly that the game becomes hard to understand.
- Add captions or text that materially explains the moment.
- Do not post filler solely to satisfy the calendar.
