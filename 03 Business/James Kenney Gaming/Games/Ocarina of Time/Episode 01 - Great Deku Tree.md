# Episode 01 — Inside the Great Deku Tree

## Viewer promise

- **Episode goal:** Begin the adventure, equip Link, complete the Great Deku Tree, and obtain the Kokiri’s Emerald.
- **Why it matters to 100%:** Complete the first dungeon while obtaining the Kokiri Sword, Deku Shield, Fairy Slingshot, Dungeon Map, Compass, first Heart Container, and Gold Skulltula Tokens #1–3.
- **Expected runtime:** Approximately 55–75 minutes; allow up to 85 minutes for the opening story, Rupee collection, or dungeon retries.
- **Next episode tease:** Leave Kokiri Forest, explore Castle Town, and sneak into Hyrule Castle to meet Princess Zelda.

## Source route

- **Authoritative guide:** [Zelda Dungeon — Inside the Great Deku Tree](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/ocarina-of-time-walkthrough/inside-the-great-deku-tree/)
- **Guide sections:** 1.1 Boy Without a Fairy, 1.2 Kokiri Forest, 1.3 Inside the Deku Tree, and 1.4 Kokiri’s Emerald
- Follow the guide in order, including Gold Skulltula Tokens #1–3 and the dungeon Map and Compass.
- The fourth Skulltula inside the dungeon is inaccessible on this first visit and will be collected later when the required item is available.

## Starting state

- **Location:** Link’s House in Kokiri Forest
- **Total life:** 3 hearts
- **Heart Pieces:** 0/36
- **Gold Skulltula Tokens:** 0/100
- **Equipment:** None
- **Run rules:** Original N64 version through emulation, no cheats, and no save states

## Route

### 1.1 — Boy Without a Fairy

- [ ] Begin from a new save file
- [ ] Record the opening narration and Link’s nightmare
- [ ] Meet Navi
- [ ] Leave Link’s House

### 1.2 — Kokiri Forest

- [ ] Meet Saria outside Link’s House
- [ ] Visit Mido and establish the sword-and-shield requirement
- [ ] Enter the Forest Training Center
- [ ] Avoid the rolling boulder
- [ ] Open the chest and obtain the Kokiri Sword
- [ ] Equip the Kokiri Sword
- [ ] Gather 40 Rupees using the guide’s Kokiri Forest route
- [ ] Enter the Kokiri Shop
- [ ] Purchase and equip the Deku Shield
- [ ] Return to Mido and gain access to the Great Deku Tree
- [ ] Defeat the Withered Deku Babas and collect Deku Sticks
- [ ] Accept the Great Deku Tree’s request
- [ ] Enter the dungeon

### 1.3 — Inside the Deku Tree

#### Upper dungeon

- [ ] Defeat or pass the first Deku Baba
- [ ] Climb the wooden structure
- [ ] Open the chest and obtain the Dungeon Map
- [ ] Defeat the first Deku Scrub by reflecting its attack
- [ ] Cross the crumbling platform
- [ ] Open the chest and obtain the Fairy Slingshot
- [ ] Shoot down the ladder and return to the main room
- [ ] Shoot the Skullwalltulas and climb to the upper floor
- [ ] Defeat or pass the Big Skulltula
- [ ] Enter the Compass room
- [ ] Activate the switch and obtain the Compass
- [ ] Collect Gold Skulltula Token #1
- [ ] Light the unlit torch and reopen the exit

#### Lower dungeon

- [ ] Jump through the center of the main-room web
- [ ] Collect Gold Skulltula Token #2 from the vines
- [ ] Collect Gold Skulltula Token #3 from the grating
- [ ] Light a Deku Stick and burn the first lower-floor web
- [ ] Defeat the second Deku Scrub
- [ ] Shoot the eye switch
- [ ] Dive onto the underwater switch
- [ ] Ride the platform beneath the spiked log
- [ ] Move the stone block beneath the doorway
- [ ] Light both torches in the torch room
- [ ] Pass through the Gohma Larva room
- [ ] Burn the far cobweb and crawl through the opening
- [ ] Push the shortcut block into the lower main room
- [ ] Carry fire to the floor web and burn it
- [ ] Defeat the three Deku Scrubs in the order 2–3–1
- [ ] Enter Queen Gohma’s room

#### Boss and completion

- [ ] Look upward to begin the Queen Gohma battle
- [ ] Stun Queen Gohma when her eye is red
- [ ] Defeat Queen Gohma
- [ ] Collect the Heart Container
- [ ] Enter the warp light and leave the dungeon

### 1.4 — Kokiri’s Emerald

- [ ] Watch the Great Deku Tree’s account of the curse and the Triforce
- [ ] Receive the Kokiri’s Emerald
- [ ] Finish after the Great Deku Tree’s final story sequence
