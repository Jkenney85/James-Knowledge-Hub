## Route

### 3.1 — Graveyard

- [ ] Travel through Kakariko Village to the Graveyard
- [ ] Play Zelda’s Lullaby before the Royal Family’s Tomb
- [ ] Enter the tomb and defeat all five Keese
- [ ] Cross the ReDead room
- [ ] Read the inscription and learn the Sun’s Song
- [ ] Return to the Graveyard
- [ ] Pull back the fourth grave from the left in the back row
- [ ] Freeze and defeat the ReDead
- [ ] Play the Sun’s Song from the platform steps
- [ ] Open the chest and collect Heart Piece #1
- [ ] Pull back the flower-marked grave in the first row
- [ ] Enter the grave and collect the free Hylian Shield
- [ ] Locate Dampe between 18:00 and 21:00
- [ ] Pay for digs until Dampe uncovers Heart Piece #2

### 3.2 — Kakariko Village

- [ ] Change to daytime with the Sun’s Song
- [ ] Gather all seven Cuccos for the Cucco Lady
- [ ] Receive Bottle #1
- [ ] Catch a Bottled Bug beneath the rock near the Graveyard entrance
- [ ] Release the bugs onto the Graveyard soft-soil patch
- [ ] Collect Gold Skulltula Token #6
- [ ] Return to Kakariko Village and change to nighttime
- [ ] Collect Token #7 from the unfinished construction wall
- [ ] Collect Token #8 from the side of the House of Skulltula
- [ ] Roll into the central tree and collect Token #9
- [ ] Collect Token #10 from the lookout-tower ladder
- [ ] Collect Token #11 from the building nearest Death Mountain
- [ ] Enter the House of Skulltula
- [ ] Claim the Adult’s Wallet reward
- [ ] Finish after claiming the wallet
