# Episode 04 — Ranch, Valley, and Lake

## Viewer promise

- **Episode goal:** Complete the Lon Lon Ranch, Gerudo Valley, and Lake Hylia portions of “The Mighty Collection.”
- **Why it matters to 100%:** Learn Epona’s Song, obtain Bottle #2, collect Heart Pieces #3–6 and Gold Skulltula Tokens #12–16, and establish the Scarecrow’s Song.
- **Expected runtime:** Approximately 80–110 minutes; allow up to 130 minutes if the Super Cucco game or fishing takes several attempts.
- **Next episode tease:** Finish the collection chapter in Kokiri Forest, the Lost Woods, Castle Town, and Kakariko Village.

## Source route

- **Authoritative guide:** [Zelda Dungeon — The Mighty Collection](https://www.zeldadungeon.net/ocarina-of-time-walkthrough/the-mighty-collection/)
- **Guide sections:** 3.3 Lon Lon Ranch, 3.4 Gerudo Valley, and 3.5 Lake Hylia
- Follow the guide in order, including the return trip to Gerudo Valley for Heart Piece #5.

## Starting state

- **Location:** Kakariko Village after claiming the Adult’s Wallet
- **Total life:** 4 hearts
- **Heart Pieces:** 2/36
- **Gold Skulltula Tokens:** 11/100
- **Key new items:** Sun’s Song, Hylian Shield, Adult’s Wallet, Bottle #1

## Route

### 3.3 — Lon Lon Ranch

- [ ] Travel to Lon Lon Ranch and change to daytime
- [ ] Complete Talon’s Super Cucco minigame
- [ ] Receive Bottle #2 containing Lon Lon Milk
- [ ] Roll into the lone tree and collect Gold Skulltula Token #12
- [ ] Speak with Malon and learn Epona’s Song
- [ ] Change to nighttime
- [ ] Collect Token #13 from the outside of the corral
- [ ] Enter the silo, move the crates, and collect Heart Piece #3

### 3.4 — Gerudo Valley

- [ ] Catch Bottled Bugs beneath the rocks near the entrance
- [ ] Pick up the Cucco and glide to the lower valley
- [ ] Release bugs onto the soft-soil patch
- [ ] Collect Gold Skulltula Token #14
- [ ] Go behind the waterfall and climb the ladder
- [ ] Collect Heart Piece #4
- [ ] Follow the river to Lake Hylia and exit north to Hyrule Field
- [ ] Return to Gerudo Valley
- [ ] Glide with the Cucco to the platform with the crate
- [ ] Roll into the crate and collect Heart Piece #5

### 3.5 — Lake Hylia

- [ ] Return through Gerudo Valley’s river to Lake Hylia
- [ ] Speak with Bonooru
- [ ] Create and record an eight-note Scarecrow’s Song
- [ ] Catch Bottled Bugs from the bush near Pierre
- [ ] Release bugs beside the Lakeside Laboratory
- [ ] Collect Gold Skulltula Token #15
- [ ] Swim to the southeastern island with two pillars
- [ ] Change to nighttime and collect Token #16
- [ ] Enter the Fishing Pond
- [ ] Catch a fish weighing at least 10 pounds
- [ ] Receive Heart Piece #6
- [ ] Finish after earning the fishing Heart Piece
