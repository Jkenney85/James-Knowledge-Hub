# Ocarina of Time — 100% series plan

## Series promise

Complete the original Nintendo 64 version of Ocarina of Time using a planned route, with no cheats and no save states. Each mostly uncut episode has one concrete objective and visibly updates the 100% checklist.

## Working definition of 100%

- Complete the story and defeat Ganon
- Complete every dungeon
- Obtain all 20 hearts, including all 36 Heart Pieces
- Collect all 100 Gold Skulltula tokens and rewards
- Obtain all equipment, capacity upgrades, and Great Fairy upgrades
- Learn all songs available in the original N64 game
- Complete the major side quests and trading sequences
- Obtain all four bottles and their key quest rewards
- Finish with visible proof from the inventory, equipment, quest-status screens, and Skulltula count

Finalize edge cases before recording in [[100 Percent Checklist]].

## Draft episode arc

The objective controls episode length. Split or combine these only when gameplay creates a better stopping point.

| Part | Working objective | Key progress |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Kokiri Forest and the Great Deku Tree | Kokiri Sword, Deku Shield, first dungeon, 3 Skulltulas |
| 2 | Castle Town and meeting Princess Zelda | Bullet Bag, Zelda’s Letter, Zelda’s Lullaby, 5 Skulltulas |
| 3 | Kakariko Village and the Graveyard | Sun’s Song, Bottle #1, Adult’s Wallet, 11 Skulltulas |
| 4 | Lon Lon Ranch, Gerudo Valley, and Lake Hylia | Epona’s Song, Bottle #2, 6 Heart Pieces, 16 Skulltulas |
| 5 | Lost Woods and collection finale | Saria’s Song, upgrades, 9 Heart Pieces, 20 Skulltulas, Stone of Agony |

Future episode numbers will be added as each Zelda Dungeon chapter is reviewed and split around natural goals and realistic runtimes.

## Episode identity

Each episode needs:

- A goal that can be understood without watching previous parts
- A reason the goal matters to the 100% run
- A visible start and finish state
- Several helpful editorial notes
- A clear next objective

## Series playlist order

Use two-digit episode numbers in metadata and filenames:

`OOT 100% - 01 - Great Deku Tree`

## Finale proof

Before entering the final battle, record:

- 20 hearts
- 100 Gold Skulltula tokens
- Full equipment screen
- Full item screen
- Quest-status screen
- Completed upgrades and songs

Then complete Ganon’s Castle and defeat Ganon.
