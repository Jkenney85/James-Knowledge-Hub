# Game selection scorecard

Score each category from 1–5.

## Selection rules

- Personal interest must be at least 4.
- The 100% definition must be explainable.
- The run must create distinct episode goals.
- Avoid committing to a game so large that the channel cannot learn from a second series during its first year.
- Prefer a timely hook, but do not choose a game solely because it is trending.

## Candidate notes

For each candidate, record:

- Why now?
- What does 100% mean?
- Estimated hours and episodes
- Three possible episode titles
- Known copyright or publisher restrictions
- Likely audience overlap with current viewers
