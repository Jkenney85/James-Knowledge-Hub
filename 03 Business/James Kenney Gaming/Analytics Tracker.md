## What the metrics mean

- **Impressions:** Whether YouTube is testing the packaging.
- **CTR:** Whether the title and thumbnail earn the click.
- **First 30 seconds:** Whether the episode immediately delivers its promise.
- **Average view duration:** The strongest practical signal for long-form watch-hour growth.
- **Returning viewers:** Whether the series is becoming a habit.
- **Subscribers gained per video:** Whether the episode makes the larger channel promise clear.

## Review discipline

- Do not compare a 48-hour number with another video’s 28-day number.
- Do not change titles or thumbnails repeatedly without enough impressions.
- Compare videos of similar age.
- Write one hypothesis before making a change.
- Record the change and date so the result can be interpreted later.
