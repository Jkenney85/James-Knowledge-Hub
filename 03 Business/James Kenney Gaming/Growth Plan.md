# Growth plan

## Objective

Build James Kenney from zero toward YouTube Partner Program eligibility, then develop it into a meaningful side-income asset.

The ad-revenue threshold to plan around is:

- 1,000 subscribers
- 4,000 valid public watch hours during the previous 12 months

The Shorts alternative is 10 million valid public Shorts views in 90 days, but that is not the primary strategy. The long-form series should generate watch hours; Shorts should create discovery and subscriber opportunities.

## Strategic position

**What makes the channel recognizable:** Goal-driven, mostly uncut 100% completion runs with visible proof of progress and a clean no-cheats/no-save-states rule.

**Why start with Ocarina of Time:** It combines nostalgia, walkthrough search intent, relaxed viewing, and timely interest following Nintendo’s June 2026 remake announcement.

**Primary risk:** Extended gameplay with no narration may struggle in both audience retention and monetization review. The answer is not noisy editing. It is stronger authorship through original route planning, goal cards, progress tracking, instructional text, chapters, results screens, and distinctive episode packaging.

## Phase 1 — Launch the system

**June 18–July 26, 2026**

- Publish Episodes 1–5 on schedule.
- Create one or two Shorts from each completed episode.
- Establish one thumbnail system and one title pattern.
- Improve the first 30 seconds every week.
- Reply to all useful comments.
- Track data without changing strategy after every upload.

Success looks like:

- Five long-form videos published
- At least seven Shorts
- No missed uploads caused by an avoidable workflow problem
- Enough impressions to begin comparing thumbnails and openings

## Phase 2 — Improve discoverability

**July 27–September 27, 2026**

- Finish roughly 13 weekly episodes total.
- Build a complete Ocarina of Time playlist in viewing order.
- Use searchable task language in titles: dungeon, item, side quest, and 100%.
- Add links from every Short to its related episode.
- Test only one packaging variable at a time.
- Use viewer comments and search terms to identify helpful overlays.

90-day planning targets:

- 13 long-form episodes
- 20–26 Shorts
- 100 subscribers
- 300 valid public watch hours
- A repeatable weekly production cycle

## Phase 3 — Complete the first flagship series

**October–December 2026**

- Finish Ocarina of Time without padding the series.
- Publish a concise finale that visibly proves 100% completion.
- Create a playlist trailer or short series recap from original footage.
- Choose the next game using [[Games/Game Selection Scorecard]].
- Keep one week of finished content in reserve.

## Phase 4 — Build toward monetization

**January–June 2027**

- Continue one strong long-form upload weekly.
- Maintain one or two Shorts only when they support the main series.
- Add one adjacent format only after the core workflow is stable.
- Review revenue paths that fit the audience: YPP ads first, then carefully selected affiliate links or memberships after demand exists.
- Do not sell Nintendo footage or images as standalone products.

## Weekly growth loop

1. Publish a clearly packaged episode with one outcome.
2. Extract the best self-contained moments into Shorts.
3. Review impressions, click-through rate, early retention, average view duration, and subscribers gained.
4. Select one improvement for the next episode.
5. Keep the series promise and upload cadence stable.

## What to optimize

| Signal | Likely issue | Next experiment |
|---|---|---|
| Low impressions | Topic or channel history | Improve searchable task wording; keep publishing |
| Impressions but low CTR | Title/thumbnail | Make the goal more specific and visual |
| Early retention drop | Opening is slow or unclear | Put goal and stakes on screen immediately |
| Good retention, few subscribers | Weak series promise | Add a subtle next-episode and subscribe prompt |
| Shorts views, no long-form traffic | Clips are disconnected | Use clips tied directly to an episode goal |
| Long videos feel repetitive | Insufficient editorial structure | Add route notes, milestone cards, and purposeful cuts |

## Guardrails

- Do not buy subscribers, views, comments, or watch time.
- Do not chase daily uploads.
- Do not create a Short unless it works as a complete moment.
- Do not let thumbnail work exceed 60 minutes during the first 90 days.
- Do not judge the channel from a single upload.
- Reassess the silent format honestly after Episodes 8 and 13.
