# Thumbnail system

## Free tool

Use Canva Free with a 1280 × 720 custom design. Save one master design and duplicate it for every episode.

## Template

Use only:

1. One strong gameplay screenshot
2. One focal subject or location
3. Two to four words naming the goal
4. A small consistent `100%` badge

Examples:

- `DEKU TREE`
- `FIRE TEMPLE`
- `100 SKULLTULAS`
- `FINAL CLEANUP`

## Style

- Darken or blur the background slightly.
- Keep Link, the boss, item, or dungeon entrance clear.
- Use cream or white type with a dark outline.
- Use gold only for the `100%` badge or one accent.
- Keep the episode number small.
- Never use a screenshot or promise that is not in the episode.

## 10-minute phone test

- Zoom out until the thumbnail is approximately phone-feed size.
- Can the subject be identified immediately?
- Can the main words be read without effort?
- Is there one obvious focal point?
- Does it look different from the previous episode?

## File naming

`oot-part-01-deku-tree-thumbnail.png`

## First 90-day rule

Spend no more than 60 minutes on a thumbnail. After enough impressions exist, improve based on click-through data instead of adding more decoration.
